The standout finding: discounting varies wildly by category

The share of products on sale at any given moment ranges from under 2% (tea) to over 22% (supplements) — a 12× difference. Categories that discount heavily are also the higher-priced ones, where a competitor's unseen markdown costs the most. In short: the more a category discounts, the more it pays to watch competitor prices — and supplements sit at the extreme of both.

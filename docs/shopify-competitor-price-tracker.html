Competitor Price Tracking for Shopify Sellers, Without the Monthly Bill

If a competitor undercuts you on Tuesday and you only notice Friday, you've lost three days of sales. Here's how to watch rival prices automatically — free, or one-time $19.

As a Shopify seller you don't need a $99/month enterprise platform to track a handful of competitors. You need to know, quickly, when a rival changes a price or sells out. This guide covers the practical options.

What to actually track (start small)

  1. Your top 10–20 revenue products — not your whole catalog.
  2. Each product's price at your top 3 competitors.
  3. Stock status — a competitor going out of stock is your window to hold or raise price.

That alone gives you more pricing awareness than most stores have.

The options

Free — DIY: Google Alerts (weak, misses fast moves), a Google Sheet with IMPORTXML (works but breaks often), or an open-source scraper you run yourself.

Monthly SaaS: Prisync ($99+/mo), Price2Spy ($39.95/mo), Pricefy (free tier up to 50 SKUs). Fine for big catalogs, overkill and pricey for a few competitors.

The number: a 12oz bag clusters at $26

Across 2,932 single-origin and blend bags, the median price is $26.00. But the median hides the real story — the spread. Half of all specialty bags sell between $18 and $32, and the market runs from a $10 floor to a $40 ceiling.

Only 5.8% is on sale — discounting is rare

Of 5,774 live variants, just 337 (5.8%) were discounted below their compare-at price. Specialty coffee holds its line; the category competes on story and freshness, not markdowns. When a competitor does discount, it stands out — and it's worth knowing the day it happens.

Methodology. Every price here was pulled live from each roaster's public product catalog (the same data their storefront serves) in July 2026 — no estimates, no surveys. "Single-bag" = variants priced $10–$40, the standard 10–12oz retail range. Medians are used throughout to avoid distortion from bundles and wholesale SKUs. The whole dataset was collected in under a minute with a small script.
